About PROSPERSEC

PROSPERSEC is a cybersecurity learning and professional development initiative founded by Prosper Chidiebere Chukwuemeka.

This platform serves as a comprehensive resource for cybersecurity knowledge, certification preparation, hands-on labs, and industry best practices. Whether you're beginning your cybersecurity journey or advancing your expertise, PROSPERSEC provides structured learning materials and practical experience.

🛡️ Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)
